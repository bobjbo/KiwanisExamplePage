Serving the Children of the World

We cooked dinner for the families at Ronald McDonald House in West County this evening. We made chili, white bean chili, mac & cheese, baked potatoes, hot dogs, salad, cornbread, brownies and cupcakes.
Thanks to our speaker today, Jennifer Bergmann, from Lindbergh Schools. She is the coordinator for our six K-Kids programs at the elementary schools. Our Club is proud to sponsor this important program.
